Projects

AI Solver for 2048

This program uses expectimax optimization as the basic AI algorithm, along with a highly-efficient 64-bit representation of the playing board. This allows it to search upwards of 10 million moves per second. The AI performs a maximize operation over all possible moves, followed by calculation of expectation values over all possible tile spawns.

iConnect - Crime Reporting App

iConnect, a Social Reporting App, provides an easy-to-use yet powerful way to report crimes in REAL-TIME by the citizens. The app has many interactive features like Pinning on Google Maps, Emergency Video Broadcast by local authorities, and one-to-one Video Chat with concerned authorities for quick resolution or crime reporting.
