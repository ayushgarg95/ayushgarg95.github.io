About me

I'm 24 years old and I was born and brought up in Delhi, India. I graduated in 2017 with a Bachelors (Hons) in Computer Engineering from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi. Since then I have been working at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer. Recently, we shifted to the new Amazon campus in Hyderabad and I totally love the cafetaria and recreational area here.

Apart from my current job, I am interested in artificial intelligence and try to follow the latest developments. Particularly, natural language understanding (NLU) fascinates me. I believe NLU is the final frontier in making next generation computers a reality, along with Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

On the lifestyle front, I try to go for the healthy food options along with my workout regime squeezed in the daily schedule. But occasionally, I indulge in paneer-filled chole bhature or the Original Legendary Burger at Hard Rock Cafe.
And I love travelling to new places and getting to know about the history and culture by visiting old town in cities all over the world.
